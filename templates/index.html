Life Path Simulations

Find out what rent you can actually afford without derailing your financial goals. See comfortable, standard, and stretch tiers — plus a live budget breakdown for any amount.

Know exactly where your first real paycheck should go. We break down your income into needs, debt payments, retirement, and lifestyle based on your actual numbers.
